Tangerine

Tangerine is MITRE's prototype that demonstrates the ideas in the Analysis Exchange [MITRE 1]. The scope of this prototype is three-fold:

  • Demonstrate the Transfer Services for exchanging raw inputs and analytic results on them
  • Provide a test client to exercise the services
  • Illustrate the process of creating analytic adapters and devising custom ontologies for specific use cases through a demonstration.
